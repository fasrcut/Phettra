Project Highlights

“Boosting Enrollment Through a Digital Awareness Campaign”

Role: Marketing Manager

Client: BELTEI University

Duration: 6 Months

Project Overview

BELTEI University was facing a decline in student inquiries. I was assigned to design and execute a campaign to increase awareness, generate high-quality leads, and strengthen the university’s digital presence.

Objectives

  • Increase online inquiries by 40% in 3 months.
  • Raise brand awareness among high school graduates (17–25).
  • Position the university as a leading modern education hub.

Strategy & Plan

  • Social Media (FB, IG, TikTok): Ran targeted ads and created student-life videos.
  • Google Ads: Optimized for “best university near me,” “business degree,” etc.
  • Landing Page: Simplified inquiry form from 8 to 4 fields & added strong CTAs.

Role: Marketing Manager

Client: RS Residence

Type: Digital + Booth Sales
